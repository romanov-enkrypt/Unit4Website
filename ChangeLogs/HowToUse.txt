C/+(Text)/-(Text)/.(Text)

T/ = Title of Change Log.
S/ = Summary of Change Log.
C/ = Concise changes (bullet points). (+)= Addition. (-)= Removal. (.)= Notice

(--) = STOP READING LOGS. MUST BE PUT!

Example:
T/Version 0.1 - Alpha Testing
S/This version is made to allow alpha testing of the game.
C/+Added Game/+Added Buy Page/-Remove Waiting For Release 
--
